TechCorp Company Profile

About TechCorp
--------------
TechCorp was founded in 2015 in Seoul, South Korea by Dr. Kim Min-jun and Sarah Chen. The company specializes in artificial intelligence solutions for enterprise customers. Our headquarters is located at 123 Innovation Street, Gangnam-gu, Seoul.

Mission Statement
-----------------
"Empowering businesses through intelligent automation and data-driven insights."

Key Facts
---------
- Founded: March 15, 2015
- Employees: 847 people globally
- Annual Revenue: $125 million (2024)
- Main Products: SmartWidget, DataFlow Pro, AI Assistant Suite
- CEO: Dr. Kim Min-jun
- CTO: Sarah Chen
- Offices: Seoul (HQ), Tokyo, San Francisco, London
